The Artist Behind the Lens

Salil Chepe is a skilled and proficient photographer, videographer, and filmmaker specialising in creative storytelling and technical excellence.

Delivering visually compelling content across product, fashion, wildlife, and automobile photography, Salil combines artistic vision with precision to enhance every project. His versatile portfolio spans commercial campaigns, high-fashion shoots, automotive photography and street photography — demonstrating adaptability and a results-driven approach.

Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Photography from MIT WPU School of Photography, Pune, Salil blends academic rigour with real-world experience working with top-rated brands and creative agencies.
